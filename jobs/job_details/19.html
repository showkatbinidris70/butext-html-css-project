Job Details

Application in prescribed form, obtainable from the office of the Registrar, are invited from the Bangladeshi nationals for filling up 01 (one) permanent post of Lecturer in the Department of Public Health in the pay scale of Tk. 22,000-53,060/- (National Pay Scale-2015).

Candidates must have a first class/a minimum CGPA 3.50 out of 4.00 in both Bachelor's (Honours) in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology/Microbiology/Pharmacy/Nutrition and Food Science/Population Sciences/ Psychology/Statistics/Applied Statistics and Master degree in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology/Microbiology/Pharmacy related subjects/ Nutrition and Food Science /Population Sciences/ Psychology/Statistics/Applied Statistics/Global Health/ Public Health along with a first division/a minimum GPA 4.25 out of 5.00 both in SSC and HSC Examinations. Requirements for either the SSC or HSC examinations may be relaxed for the candidates who have obtained the first position/the highest CGPA in both Bachelor and Master degrees. Candidates with a Ph.D. in Global Health/Public Health and research publication(s) in a quality journal may get preference. Relevant notes and conditions apply. (For details, see the Dhaka University Website).

Eight copies of application together with attested copies of certificates, testimonials, mark sheets and proof of experience along with a Pay Order/Bank Draft of the value of Tk. 750/-(seven hundred fifty) only, payable to the Registrar, University of Dhaka, should reach him on or before 06/05/2024. Candidates already in service must apply through proper channel.
